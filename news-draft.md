  Link: https://www.scientificamerican.com/article/urban-heat-islands-mean-warming-will-be-worse-in-cities/

- **Yale News / ScienceDaily: "For Some Urban Areas, Warming Climate is Only Half the Threat"** (November 2019)
  Research covered by Yale News and ScienceDaily. Highlighted finding that urban heat island warming will probably be equivalent to about half the warming caused by climate change by 2050, and up to twice as strong in some locations.
